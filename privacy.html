Privacy Policy — Quran Time

Last updated: 21 January 2026

Quran Time (“the App”) is designed to provide a calm, respectful, and privacy-focused experience centered on Quran-inspired audio stories for reflection, relaxation, sleep, and family-friendly listening.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how data is handled.

1. Information We Collect

Quran Time does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as names, addresses, phone numbers, or precise location data.

The App may collect limited, non-personal information required to operate core features, subscriptions, and app stability.

2. Analytics and App Diagnostics

Quran Time uses Firebase Analytics to collect aggregated and anonymized usage data.

This data is used solely to:

  • Understand feature usage
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Detect crashes and technical issues

Analytics data may include:

  • App screens viewed
  • General interaction patterns
  • Device and app version information

This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

3. Advertising (Free Version)

Quran Time may display advertisements to users who do not have an active subscription.

Advertising is provided via Google AdMob.

  • Ads may be contextual or non-personalized where required by law
  • Advertising identifiers may be used by AdMob for ad delivery and measurement
  • We do not sell user data
  • We do not perform ad retargeting outside the app

Users with an active subscription do not see advertisements.

4. Subscriptions and Purchases

Quran Time offers optional subscriptions to unlock premium, ad-free content.

Subscriptions and payments are processed securely by:

  • Apple App Store (iOS)
  • Google Play Store (Android)

Quran Time does not store or process payment information.

Subscription status is handled via RevenueCat and is used only to determine access to premium features.

5. Data Stored on Your Device

The App may store certain information locally on your device to enable core functionality, such as:

  • Audio playback state
  • Sleep timer settings
  • Ambient sound preferences
  • Cached audio or images

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

6. Children’s Privacy

Quran Time includes a Kids Mode with age-appropriate, family-friendly content.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children
  • We do not display interest-based advertising in Kids Mode
  • We do not perform behavioral profiling of children

All analytics data is collected in an anonymous and aggregated manner.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

Limited, anonymized data may be processed by trusted service providers (Firebase, AdMob, RevenueCat) solely for the purposes described in this policy.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in features or legal requirements.

Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
